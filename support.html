There are multiple ways to help SEED grow:

Shedding Light

Our biggest factor in our growth is community. We can start with marketing and advertisement, but we are going to need direct help. By pressing the button below you will be given a copy of a script that we ask you to send to your local school districts. By getting in touch with representatives from schools directly, we are optimizing our chances of implementing more natural ways of growing and learning into school systems across the world. This may be crucial to our growth and with every message that gets sent out, we are building a community from within to cultivate children to higher stages of evolution.

Watering the Plant

With community funding we can advance our research in schools, provide our service with better equipment, and advance towards new projects surrounding the ideas of art, celebration, spirit, technology, and communion . As a california non-profit organization it is our duty to use collected funds for the purpose of advancing our mission.

Maintaining the Soil

Those who are closest to the roots of this process, stablizing our growth from the inside, are the one's who become apart of our team. We are looking for involvement across the world and we are willing to meet your needs to spread seed into schools, workspaces, and public hostings. Like soil, our team although made of many individual pieces of the Earth, are packed tightly to stregthen the success of our growth.
